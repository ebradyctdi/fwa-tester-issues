How It Works

  • Daily: Email sent every day at your selected time
  • Weekly: Email sent every Monday at your selected time
  • Monthly: Email sent on the 1st of each month at your selected time
  • Open Issues Only: Lists all unresolved issues. If none, sends a "all clear" confirmation.
  • Full History: Includes all issues (open + resolved) as a summary report.
  • Schedules are managed via Google Apps Script triggers. Use "Send Test Email" to verify.
